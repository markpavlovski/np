Special Instructions for Groupon Customers

This promotion was offered as a way to introduce new customers to Nashoba Paddler. We hope you enjoy your visit with us and will become one of our regular customers. In order to help things go as smoothly as possible please help us by reading the following:

  1. Reservations are required when using your Groupon. When making your reservations, please provide us with your Groupon number(s). Please be aware that rentals are based on availability.
  2. The state requires that we collect sales tax. Please be aware that you will be asked to pay the sales tax on the amount of the value of the rental you've requested.
  3. Cancellations must be made 3 days in advance. If there is a Weather Warning such as dangerous thunderstorms, high winds, flooding, or heavy rain, you may reschedule on the day of your reservation.
  4. Please see the general Rental Rules.
