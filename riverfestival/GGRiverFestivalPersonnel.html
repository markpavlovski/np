River Festival Personnel

Personnel

There are two goals with regard to finding volunteers for this event. The first is that we attract enough volunteers that no one person is overwhelmed and will not want to do this again. The second is that we attract as many new people as possible. This is seen as a way to garner interest in the Groton Greenway Committee.

  • Event Coordinator - overall responsibility for the budget, personnel and schedule.
  • Site Coordinator - Secure permit for selected site. (Assume Petapawag launch area.) Notify invested parties (notably the bass boaters).
  • Parking - Responsible arranging for transportation from the NRWA if necessary. Arrange withe local police for detail.
  • Environmental Education - Responsible for working with various government agenices (state, schools?) and nonprofits (notably the NRWA) to have exhibits regarding the environmental education.
  • Volunteer Coordinator - Organize the larger set of volunteers required on the day of the festival.
  • Music - Responsbile for working with musicians and sound crew to supply music for the festival. Depending on the budget, this may be minimal. Knowledge of copyright issues are a plus.
  • Card Board Canoe Race Coordinator - Responsible for securing Card Board and Duct Tape for the race and other planning involved with running this event.
  • Children Activities - Coordinate various activities including art activities. Depending on budget and ideas this may expand.
  • Marketing - Responsible for press releases and other ways to spread the word.
  • Grant Writer -using existing Grant Proposals as a model, submit to the various availble grants to secure funding to support the budget.
  • Fund Raiser -contact local area businesses to solicit interest in donating to support this event.
  • Graphic Designer - Provide or arrange for artwork for any flyers, banners or marketing information.
  • Food Coordinator - work with various vendors to provide food for the event (NOT free). (For example; Boy Scouts)
